Interesting Facts About Frogs:

  • Frogs do not need to drink water as they absorb it through their skin.
  • Frogs shed their skin regularly depending on species from as often as every day or up to a few times each month.
  • Many frogs eat their shedded skin, recycling nutrients and minerals.
  • Poison dart frogs are one of the most poisonous animals alive. Their skin secretes a toxic substance.
  • The goliath frog can grow up to 1 foot long and weigh more than 7 pounds.
  • Some frogs can jump over 20 times their own body length. Which can be the equivalent of a human jumping 30 meters.
